I am a PhD candidate in Mathematics at the Applied Functional Analysis Group of TU Berlin working mainly on Deep Learning, Compressed Sensing and Image Processing. My current project is centered on the use of Deep Convolutional Neural Networks for learning tomographic reconstructions.

I form a part of the graduate program Berlin Mathematical School, being a Phase II scholarship holder. I am also the main developer of the Multidimensional Signal Processing julia library Shearlab.jl. More recently I implemented a tensorflow front-end of this library, called tfShearlab.
