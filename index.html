About

I'm a Mexican mathematician and AI researcher based in Munich. I hold a PhD in Mathematics from LMU Munich and dual BSc degrees in Mathematics and Physics from UNAM, both with near-perfect GPAs. My research bridges applied harmonic analysis, deep learning, and inverse problems — with applications in 3D computer vision, biomedical imaging, and computational physics.

Beyond academia, I co-founded AngioWave Imaging at the intersection of AI and vascular imaging, and Colmenas, an AI engineering studio serving Latin American industries. Previously, I led AI at NAZCA, a leading LatAm VC fund.
